Vasileios Nakos, Ph.D. | Harvard University

Bio

I am a fourth year ph.D. student in the Theory of Computation group of Harvard University, advised by Prof. Jelani Nelson. My research till now has been focused on compressed sensing and streaming algorithms. You can contact me if you have interest or questions in any of my papers.
